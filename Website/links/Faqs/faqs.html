What is the Industrial Arts strand in the Senior High School (SHS) TechVoc track?
The Industrial Arts strand under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track provides students with the necessary knowledge and skills they need for employment. These include technical and manual skills in Carpentry and Construction, Electrical and Electronics, Automotive and Driving, Repair and Installation, Welding, and Plumbing.
