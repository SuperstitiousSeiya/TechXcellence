Industrial Arts

What is Industrial Arts?

The Industrial Arts Strand in the TLE track focused on providing students with knowledge and skills related to different technical and vocational fields, particularly those related to industrial processes and craftsmanship. The curriculum typically covered areas such as carpentry, metal works, electrical installation and maintenance, plumbing, and other practical skills relevant to industries like construction, manufacturing, and maintenance.
